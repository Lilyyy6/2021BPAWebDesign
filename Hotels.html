Location

The location of the BPA NLC is located at the Swan and Dolphin Hotels in Orlando, Florida. Disney World is quite large and has many things to do. Click on each button to see details of your stay.

Walt Disney World Bus Service

Disney World has a bus service free to guests. There are bus stops both at the Swan and Dolphin Hotel. The buses run from 45 minutes before opening and 1 hour after closing.

Guidlines

All strollers must be foldable and stowed out of the aisle.

Car Rental

Rent a car through Enterprise, Alamo, or National by booking a vehicle online or calling (407) 824-3470. The Care Care Center is located 1000 West Car Care Drive Bay Lake, Florida 32830, or near Magic Kingdom theme park auto plaza.

Arrange a complimentary shuttle pick up to the Care Care Center by calling (407) 824-3470. The pick up location is located outside the lobby.

If renting through Enterprise, Alamo, or National, schedule at least one hour prior to pick up time and be waiting at the pickup location at the appointed time.

Minnie Van Service

This personal ride service is avalible through the Lyft app. Each car holds 6 guests and the price is based on distance traveled. This service is avalible at the Swan and Dolphin hotels between 6am and 12am. Minnie Van Disney Website

Request a Minnie Van accessable vehicle by calling (407) 828-3500 45 to 60 minutes prior to needing the vehicle. Disney Minnie Van vehicles can accomidate to standard wheelchairs, motorized vehicles, and electric conveyance vehicles (ECVs).

Minnie Van Airport Service

This ride service takes you to and from the Orlando International Airport to your Disney Resort Hotel. Arrange a one-way or round-trip up to 6 months in advance by calling (407) WDW-PLAY or (407) 939-7529. It runs from 6am to 11:59pm at $115 per trip, not including a tip for the driver.

Each car holds 6 guests, 6 medium-sized suitcases, and has 3 car seats avalible for free use. The pickup location at Orlando International Airport is located by the baggage claim escalators.

Dining Plans

Depending on your situation, it might be a good idea to get a dinning plan. Guests need to purchase a Disney Resort Hotel Package that includes the choosen plan.

Disney Quick Service Dining Plan

Price per night:

Adult: $55

Child (3-9yrs): $26

Includes 2 Quick-Service Meals and 2 snacks or non-alcohlic beverages per night of stay and a Resort Refillable Mug. The full menu plan can be found on the Disney Quick Service Dining Plan Page.

Disney Dining Plan

Price per night:

Adult: $94.61

Child: $35

Includes one Quick-Service Meal, one Table-Service meal, and one snack per night of stay and a Resort Refillable Mug. The full menu plan can be found on the Disney Dining Plan Page.

Disney Deluxe Dining Plan

Price per night:

Adult: $144

Child: $47.50

Includes 3 meals, any combination of Quick-Service or Table-Service, and 2 snacks or target="_blank"non-alcoholic drinks per night of stay and a Resort Refillable Mug. The full menu plan can be found on the Disney Deluxe Dining Plan Page.

Outside Food Options

Disney and other Resorts are netorious for being expensive. Luckily, you can order food from outside Disney World and save money

GrubHub

GrubHub is a food delivery app that delivers inside Walt Disney World. They have 597 resturants avalible for Disney World Delivery ranging from classic Nation-wide fast-food chains like Taco Bell and SUBWAY to more casual dinning like Peacock Alley and Harvest Bistro.

You can find a full list of avalible resturants on the GrubHub Website
